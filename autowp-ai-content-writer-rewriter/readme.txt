== Installation ==

1. Upload the `autowp` directory to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Configure the plugin by going to the AutoWP menu that appears in your admin menu.




== Frequently Asked Questions ==
AutoWP - Content Writer & Rewriter Plugin - Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

= What is AutoWP - Content Writer & Rewriter Plugin? = 
AutoWP is a powerful WordPress plugin designed to streamline your content creation process. It harnesses advanced AI technology to fetch and rewrite content from RSS feeds, WordPress and Google News sites. Additionally, it can create original content from scratch, complete with SEO-friendly titles, tags, and thumbnail images. Contents generated by AutoWP got +80 RankMath score in average!

= How does AutoWP rewrite content? =
AutoWP utilizes cutting-edge AI algorithms to rewrite fetched content, ensuring it becomes unique while maintaining its relevancy and coherence. The plugin restructures sentences, replaces synonyms, and uses advanced natural language processing techniques to create fresh and engaging content.

= Can I use AutoWP for creating original content? =
Absolutely! AutoWP\'s AI can generate entirely original content tailored to your preferences. You can provide custom prompts for the AI or use the system\'s default prompts to initiate the content creation process.

= Are the titles, tags, and images SEO optimized? =
Yes, AutoWP takes SEO seriously. Every title, tag, and image it produces is crafted to be search engine friendly, ensuring that your content has the best chance of ranking high in search results.

= Can I edit the images automatically added to the content? =
Yes, you have the option to automatically edit the images. AutoWP provides features such as adding text overlay and watermarks to the images, allowing you to customize them to align with your brand or style.

= Is the content generation process fully automated? =
Yes, AutoWP is designed to be flexible. You can choose between fully automated content generation or a manual approach. If you prefer automation, you can set specific time intervals for the plugin to produce new content regularly.

= Can I use AutoWP with my WordPress theme and plugins? =
AutoWP is designed to be compatible with most standard WordPress themes and plugins. However, we recommend testing it with your specific setup to ensure seamless integration.

= Is there customer support available for the plugin? =
Yes, we offer dedicated customer support to assist you with any issues or questions related to AutoWP. Feel free to reach out to our support team, and we\'ll be happy to help you.

= Is AutoWP suitable for all types of websites? =
Yes, AutoWP can be used on various types of WordPress websites, including blogs, online magazines, e-commerce sites, and more. Its content creation capabilities can benefit any site that seeks high-quality, SEO-optimized content.

= Are there any subscription fees for using AutoWP? =
Yes, AutoWP operates on a subscription-based model, offering various plans to suit different needs. You can choose the plan that best fits your requirements and budget.
